AWARDS

Award Categories

Best Radio Drama

The group that demonstrates the best storytelling and cohesive integration of script, voice, sound and vision.

Best Audio Effects

The group with the most outstanding ambient sound, background music and audio manipulation.

Best Visual Effects

The group with the most intricately designed animation, drawings, shading and digital effects.

Best Voice Acting

The group in which actresses are able to convey emotion, character and authenticity most proficiently.

Best Script

The group that has the best dialogue, structure, character development and narrative.

Best Promotion

The group with the best bulletin design and promotional methods. This will be voted on during Drama Night.
