Program Highlights

1. Radio Drama Exhibition

Explore the creative journey of each AD radio drama group through mobile boards showcasing their year-long working process. Meet the representatives behind the scenes to hear their unique experiences and ideas.

2. Live Stage Performances

A series of original productions from the DGS Drama Ensemble and participating schools: Maryknoll Convent School (Secondary Section) and SKH Tsang Shiu Tim Secondary School. Includes a medley performance by the DGS Rope Skipping team and winning acts from the Drama Ensemble's musical singing competition.

3. Prize Presentation for Radio Drama Production

Official announcement of the Best Radio Drama Group Award and showcase of the winning production video.

4. Drama Appreciation Symposium

Closing speeches by the Organising Committee and Drama Ensemble chairladies sharing their experiences and takeaways, followed by a souvenir presentation for all participating schools.
